Prescriptive Analytics

Data, properly leveraged, holds the answer to what decisions you should make.

Alerts

The Tachyus platform intelligently alerts you of opportunities, abnormalities, and data problems so that instead of manually sifting through vast quantities of data, you can focus on the most important challenges and questions for your operations.

Recommendations

In many cases, our analysis engine can go beyond alerts and actually provide lucrative recommendations such as which workover to perform next, when to steam a well, optimal injection rates throughout a waterflood, or where to drill.

Optimization

The oil patch is all about capital allocation, investment and risk management. Tachyus users leverage our optimization algorithms to mitigate risk while determining the best possible use of capital across ongoing operations and new asset development.

Predictive Analytics

To know the future is to control the future. Reliable predictions mean better decisions.

Physical Modeling

We leverage fundamental physical principles to model equipment operations, production trends, and reservoir dynamics. Our scientific approach helps us to predict asset failure, minimize downtime, and turn big data into valuable business insight.

Machine Learning

As our software ingests more data, it becomes more valuable to oil and gas producers. Each additional data point and user action teaches Tachyus more and adds to the core data infrastructure and algorithms powering the Tachyus platform.

Financial Predictions

Tachyus’s predictive algorithms compute expected response and ROI for workovers, pump changes, injection rate modifications, drilling plans, and alternative completion parameters. Accurate predictions means you act on superior opportunities.

Descriptive Analytics

Data must be explored, visualized, and comprehended in the decision-making process.

Charting

Large data sets are useless without powerful visualization tools. We provide a wide range of visualizations to empower engineers to conceptualize the significance of data. Easily configure your favorite views and instantly select target data sets.

Extensibility

We provide default views we think you will love and the flexibility to modify them or create your own. You can use intuitive configurations to quickly edit every detail including colors, shapes, sizes, visualization types, and data sources.

Cross-Platform

Access Tachyus reports on huge command center displays, tablets in the field or your mobile phone while at home. It has never been easier to keep track of your oil and gas assets on the go and to share reports and analysis with colleagues.

Integration

Data is useless for decision-making without an integrated context.

Operations

Petroleum engineers can track and manage contractor operations, prepare wellbore schematics based on drilling and workover history, organize maintenance communications via email and calls, and share safety and regulatory reports.

Development

Geologists and reservoir engineers combine public benchmark data and proprietary logs, forecasts, cores, and geophysical models. Crucial technical staff should spend their time analyzing pivotal data sets, not manually integrating disparate data sources.

Source of Truth

Tachyus serves as a “digital well-file” to access a complete integrated timeline for any asset. Geologists, contractors, engineers, and accountants quickly integrate and share, drilling, completions, production, maintenance and financial data.

Measurement

The foundation of effective decision-making is collecting accurate and frequent data.

SCADA

We integrate with your existing SCADA infrastructure to measure critical well, tank, and generator parameters such as pressures, temperatures, fluid levels, and flow rates. The Tachyus platform automatically cleans incoming data streams and alerts you of abnormal or missing measurements to ensure optimal data for analytical purposes.

Mobile Applications

Gone are the days of retyping handwritten measurements and notes. Our iOS applications make it easy for pumpers to record data electronically in the field and provides them immediate access to all relevant field data. GPS integration, notifications, and automatic wireless sync make field data entry a breeze.

Research

We develop novel sensor technology that is affordable for even the lowest-producing stripper wells. Areas of research include automatic fluid level gauging, downhole pressure gauges, cut sensors, two and three phase flow meters, and systems that can digitize analog gauges with no modification of existing hardware.
